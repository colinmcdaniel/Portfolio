About

Here is some background information about me.

Music

I have been playing the drums since age 2 and playing professionally since I was 13 years old. After high school, I attended the Brubeck Institute Fellowship jazz program at University of the Pacific. I've recorded an album of original compositons, released under Jazzschool Records, and had the opportunity to perform with a Grammy Award winner and various various Grammy Award nominees. He has won over 15 awards and 5 scholarships for jazz performance and composition, including the Louis Armstrong Jazz Award and three DownBeat Magazine Student Music Awards.

Education

Colin moved to Los Angeles to attend UCLA, and graduated summa cum laude in Cognitive Science with a Specialization in Computing. Since graduating, he has been involved in neurobiology research and web development. While at UCLA, he learned programming languages such as C++ (computer science fundamentals, data structures, algorithms), Java (GUI applications, Android development), and MATLAB (computer-based experiments, gathering data). While at UCLA, I worked in a cognitive psychology laboratory, in which I taught myself some Python and ran human subjects in various experiments.

Hobbies

Colin's hobbies include listening to podcasts, reading, watching movies, meditation, playing board games, and hanging out with friends. Some of his interests include psychology, artificial intelligence, neuroscience, philosophy, and statistics. He also has an interest in filmmaking, and some of his films were Official Selections at various local and international film festivals.
